Niyamat

Niyamat is a mental health awareness initiative I founded in March 2020 along with a few engineering graduates from Manipal Institute of Technology.
With a podcast featuring some of the country's most renowned psychologists, we aim to initiate a healthy mental health dialogue - one audience at a time.

MIND BASTI

MIND BASTI is a stress management project through a purely psychological lens; navigating through concepts such as the stress bucket, the feelings wheel and grounding techniques such as the 4-7-8 breathing pattern and the 5-4-3-2-1 method.
